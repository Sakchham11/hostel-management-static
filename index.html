Heathy & Testy food services

The mess facilities shall be integrated hostel-wise, therefore each hostel shall have its own mess. The mess will operate under high standards of food quality and hygiene. A student committee of residents of that hostel shall monitor and provide continuous feedback of functioning of the mess. The mess shall offer a rich menu of food items catering to needs of all students. It shall have enough flexibility to in case a student desires to have something off-menu using coupons and cashless system of entries.

Why Hostelry

Necessity

We have got five hostels in our university, which consist of four boy’s hostel and one girl’s hostel. All these hostels at present are managed manually by the hostel office. The Registration form verification to the different data processing are done manually. Thus there are a lot of repetitions which can be easily avoided. And hence there is a lot of strain on the person who are running the hostel and software’s are not usually used in this context.This particu- lar project deals with the problems on managing a hostel and avoids the problems which occur when carried manually Identification of the drawbacks of the existing system leads to the designing of computerized system that will be compatible to the existing system with the system which is more user friendly and more GUI oriented. We can improve the efficiency of the system, thus overcome the drawbacks of the existing system.

Objective

The main objective behind preparing this project is that to give hostel a complete facility that a hostel managerial team wants. It even helps the user to handle the managing system of the hostel properly. It also gives the customer an easier to have booking and other related activities hostel. Besides this, there are many objectives like:  To manipulate the Hostel transaction with instant confirmation.  To save time.  To increase efficiency of the students.  For fast access of mess bills and notices.  Indicate the room availability and the location of the room.  If the visitor is satisfied with the room location, then confirm the booking; and upon the confirmation print the payment details and other necessary constraints.

Feasibility

A feasibility study is conducted to select the best system that meets performance requirements. This entails an identification description, an evaluation of the candidate system, and the selection of the best system for the job. The system required performance is defined by a statement of constraints, the identification of specific system objectives, and a description of outputs. The key consideration in feasibility analysis are-  Economic Feasibility  Technical Feasibility  Operational Feasibility  Behavioral Feasibility
